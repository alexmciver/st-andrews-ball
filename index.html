The St Andrew's Ball is the highlight of the winter reeling season in London, taking place in the magnificent Porchester Hall in Notting Hill.

Dinner is served in the lower dining room, before the dancing begins on a proper, full-size ballroom floor. As always, Frank Reid and his wonderful band provide the music throughout the evening.

Bring a party for Dinner and Dancing, or join us after dinner for Dancing only.

One of London's finest event venues, Porchester Hall provides the perfect setting for a grand evening of Scottish country dancing.

The full-size ballroom floor accommodates the grandest reels, while the lower dining room provides an intimate setting for dinner before the dancing begins.
